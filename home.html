Revolution, Reaction, Reform

Nellie Bly's story perfectly embodies the 2025 National History Day theme. Her groundbreaking career demonstrates how one person's revolutionary actions can spark widespread reaction and lead to lasting reform.

Revolution

Bly revolutionized journalism by pioneering undercover investigative reporting and breaking gender barriers in a male-dominated profession.

Reaction

Her exposés sparked immediate public outrage, government investigations, and debates about women's roles in society.

Reform

Her work led to concrete changes: improved mental health care, journalism standards, and expanded opportunities for women.

Why Nellie Bly Matters

In an era of rapid social change, Nellie Bly proved that journalism could be a powerful force for justice. Her fearless pursuit of truth not only exposed injustices but created a blueprint for using media to drive meaningful social progress—lessons that remain relevant today.
